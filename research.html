Research Centers & Institutes

Advanced Technology Innovation Center (ATIC)

Director: Dr. Maria Santos

Leading research in artificial intelligence, quantum computing, and emerging technologies. The center houses state-of-the-art computing facilities and collaborates with industry leaders worldwide.

AI/ML Quantum Computing Robotics

Environmental Research Institute (ERI)

Director: Dr. Juan Dela Cruz

Dedicated to environmental conservation, climate change research, and sustainable development. The institute operates field stations across the Philippines and collaborates with international environmental organizations.

Climate Science Conservation Sustainability

Health Sciences Research Institute (HSRI)

Director: Dr. Ana Rodriguez

Advancing medical research, biotechnology, and healthcare innovations. The institute features advanced laboratories, clinical research facilities, and partnerships with leading hospitals.

Biotechnology Precision Medicine Public Health

Space Technology Research Center (STRC)

Director: Dr. Carlos Luna

Pioneering space exploration technologies, satellite development, and aerospace engineering. The center operates satellite ground stations and collaborates with international space agencies.

Satellite Systems Space Propulsion Aerospace

Active

Developing a quantum-secured satellite communication system for ultra-secure data transmission. This project aims to revolutionize secure communications in space.

Principal Investigator: Dr. Maria Santos

Funding: $2.5M (Department of Science and Technology)

Duration: 2023-2026

Partners: Philippine Space Agency, NASA

Duration: 2022-2024

"Sustainable Campus Development: A Comprehensive Framework"

Authors: Dela Cruz, J., Rodriguez, A., & Santos, M.

Environment
