Main Campus

ISCP Main Campus

Manila, Philippines

Our flagship campus located in the heart of Manila, featuring state-of-the-art facilities, modern classrooms, advanced laboratories, and comprehensive student services. This campus serves as the administrative center and houses our main academic programs.

Key Features:
  • Central Library with 100,000+ volumes
  • Advanced Computer Laboratories
  • Research Centers and Innovation Hub
  • Student Center and Recreation Facilities
  • Modern Auditorium and Conference Rooms
