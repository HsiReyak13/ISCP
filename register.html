Registration is open to qualified students who meet the admission requirements set forth by ISCP. All information provided during registration must be accurate and truthful.

Students must maintain satisfactory academic progress and comply with all institutional policies, rules, and regulations. Failure to meet academic standards may result in academic probation or dismissal.

Students are expected to maintain high standards of academic integrity and personal conduct. Any form of academic dishonesty, including plagiarism and cheating, will not be tolerated.
