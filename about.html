About ISCP

Discover our story, mission, and commitment to excellence in education.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for personal growth and national development. We strive to create an inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, innovation, and social responsibility.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, research innovation, and community service. We envision ISCP as a catalyst for positive change, producing competent professionals who contribute to the advancement of society and the nation.

Our History

A journey of growth and excellence in education

2022 - Foundation

International State College of the Philippines was established with a vision to provide accessible, quality education to Filipino students. The institution began with a commitment to academic excellence and community service.

2023 - Growth & Expansion

ISCP expanded its academic programs and facilities, establishing partnerships with industry leaders and educational institutions. The college introduced new degree programs and enhanced its research capabilities.

2024 - Academic Excellence

The institution achieved significant milestones in academic performance, research output, and community engagement. ISCP strengthened its commitment to producing competent and ethical professionals.

2025 - Future Forward

ISCP continues to evolve and adapt to the changing educational landscape, embracing technology and innovation while maintaining its core values of excellence, integrity, and service.
