Discover the journey that shaped ISCP into a premier educational institution

International State College of the Philippines was established with a vision to provide world-class education to Filipino students.

2000

First Graduation Ceremony

ISCP celebrated its first batch of graduates, marking a significant milestone in the institution's history.

Present

Leading the Future

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for personal growth and national development. We strive to create an inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, innovation, and social responsibility.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, research innovation, and community service. We envision ISCP as a catalyst for positive change, producing competent professionals who contribute to the advancement of society and the nation.

Goals & Objectives

Student Development

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our educational mission

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of education, research, and service.

Integrity

We uphold ethical principles, honesty, and transparency in all our actions and decisions.

Respect

We value diversity, promote inclusivity, and treat everyone with dignity and respect.

Innovation

We encourage creativity, critical thinking, and the pursuit of new knowledge and solutions.

Service

We are committed to serving our community and contributing to national development.

Global Perspective

We prepare students to be globally competitive while maintaining Filipino values and identity.

Quality Policy

Our commitment to continuous improvement

ISCP is committed to providing quality education through continuous improvement of our academic programs, research capabilities, and community services. We strive to meet and exceed the expectations of our stakeholders while maintaining the highest standards of excellence in all our endeavors.
