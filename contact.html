Find Us

Visit our campus

Interactive Campus Map

Our campus is located in the heart of Manila, easily accessible by public transportation.

Address: 123 University Avenue, Manila, Philippines 1000
Nearest MRT Station: Ayala Station (5-minute walk)
Bus Routes: Routes 1, 3, 5, and 7 stop nearby

Getting Here

By MRT

Take the MRT to Ayala Station. Exit and walk 5 minutes north on University Avenue.

By Bus

Take any bus on routes 1, 3, 5, or 7. Get off at the University Avenue stop.

By Car

Parking is available on campus. Enter from University Avenue or Campus Drive.

By Bike

Bike racks are available at all campus entrances. Secure parking provided.
